My Projects

Reflect.

Ever feel like you need more motivation? Need somewhere to share your accomplishments? Well, Reflect. is the place to go. Reflect. is a platform created using React Native, Expo, TypeScript, CSS, and Node.js/Express.js, providing a social media platform for users to find others who share similar goals and accomplishments and spread their own motivation, too. Stay motivated and always remember to try your best :)

And yes, this is a work in progress...

DoorSense

An embedded smart door home security system built using a variety of technologies, including Raspberry Pi and a mobile iOS app to connect it all together! While my amazing group members handled the rest, I planned out the project, designed the mobile application, and created all of the front-end for the iOS application using React Native, Expo, TypeScript, and CSS. And! I spent 9 hours creating the demo video linked below!

ShopSmart

Busy with life and still need to get groceries? No need to worry anymore - just use ShopSmart! Add your grocery list and an optmized shopping trip will be auto-generated for you. Just follow it, and you'll be saving both money and time! Created by students who struggle with the same problem, using React Native, Expo, TypeScript, CSS and MariaDB. Check out my Figma design below!

Pocket Pantry

Ever have a hard time deciding on what food to make? Well, here's a solution! Add all of your food ingredients in the app, and just scroll through the auto-generate recipes to find one you like! Pssst... check out the demo video I created in 28 minutes with 2 minutes to spare before the deadline :)

Generative Art

Generative Art is a website made using JavaScript. Using your mouse, you can create different-shaped eclipses in different shades of green and blue by clicking and dragging the cursor around.

P.S. The background changes colour when you press any key on your keyboard!

Glide IT

Glide IT is an online game similar to Flappy Bird. You can control the square using arrow keys to avoid obstacles. Fork the Replit and run it to play :)

My personal highscore is 25921. Try to beat it - I believe in you!

Life Simulator

Want to see how it feels to live a different life? Try out this life simulator, a text-based game built with Python, where you can choose from various options what to do at each stage of your life. See where you end up!
